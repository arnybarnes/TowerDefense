This is a wave-based tower defense game set in modern and near-future military environments. Players strategically place and upgrade defensive units (towers) to stop waves of increasingly difficult enemies from reaching a protected objective. The game emphasizes tactical placement, resource management, progression, and replayability.

⸻

2. Goals & Objectives

Player Goals
	•	Prevent enemies from reaching the base.
	•	Earn in-game currency through successful defense.
	•	Unlock, upgrade, and optimize towers.
	•	Progress through increasingly challenging maps and waves.

Product Goals
	•	Deliver a highly replayable tower defense experience.
	•	Balance accessibility for new players with depth for advanced players.
	•	Encourage long-term engagement via progression systems and unlockables.
	
	3. Target Audience
	•	Primary: Casual to mid-core gamers who enjoy strategy and tower defense games.
	•	Secondary: Younger players and multiplayer-oriented users who prefer cooperative or competitive play.
	•	Platform Audience: Players familiar with sandbox or community-driven game platforms.

⸻

4. Core Gameplay Loop
	1.	Start a match on a selected map.
	2.	Receive initial resources.
	3.	Place towers along enemy paths.
	4.	Enemies spawn in waves.
	5.	Towers automatically attack enemies.
	6.	Player upgrades or adds towers between waves.
	7.	Earn rewards for wave completion.
	8.	Repeat until victory or base defeat.

⸻

5. Key Features

5.1 Towers
	•	Types: Infantry, vehicles, artillery, air defense, support units.
	•	Attributes:
	•	Damage
	•	Range
	•	Fire rate
	•	Special effects (slow, splash damage, armor piercing)
	•	Upgrades:
	•	Tiered upgrade paths
	•	Visual and performance changes

5.2 Enemies
	•	Multiple enemy classes with increasing difficulty.
	•	Variants include:
	•	Fast units
	•	Armored units
	•	Boss enemies
	•	Each wave introduces new mechanics or combinations.

5.3 Maps
	•	Military-themed zones (bases, cities, deserts, war zones).
	•	Fixed enemy paths with strategic choke points.
	•	Environmental variety affecting placement and tactics.

5.4 Economy
	•	Currency earned per enemy defeated and wave completed.
	•	Strategic trade-offs between saving resources and upgrading early.
	•	Possible bonus rewards for flawless waves.

6. Progression System
	•	Player Progression:
	•	Unlock new towers and upgrades.
	•	Access higher difficulty maps.
	•	Session Progression:
	•	Increasing wave difficulty.

	•	Persistent unlocks across sessions.

7. Game Modes (Optional / Extended)
	•	Standard Mode: Classic wave-based gameplay.
	•	Hard / Endless Mode: Infinite waves with escalating difficulty.
	•	Co-op Mode: Multiple players defend together, sharing or managing resources.
